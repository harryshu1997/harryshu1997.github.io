Frequently Asked Questions

This page addresses common questions I receive from prospective students, collaborators, and the general public. If you don't find your question answered here, please feel free to reach out directly.
For Prospective PhD Students
What research areas are you most interested in?
My primary research interests include machine learning systems, parrallel computing, and AI applications. I'm particularly excited about projects that bridge theory and practice, and that have potential for real-world impact.
For Collaborators & Industry Partners
Are you available for collaboration?
I am open to collaboration opportunities that align with my research interests. Please reach out with specific details about the project and timeline. My interests are AI, MLSys, VR, AR, and MetaVerse.
