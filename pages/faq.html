Frequently Asked Questions

About my work, interests, and collaboration.

Are you looking for internship opportunities?
Yes! I am actively looking for Research Scientist or Software Engineer internships. My expertise lies in LLM inference acceleration, Edge AI, and System Optimization. Feel free to contact me via email.
How can we collaborate on research?
I am always open to collaboration, especially on topics related to Mobile AI, NPU optimization, and Diffusion Models. Please shoot me an email with your ideas!
What hardware do you use for experiments?
My current research focuses heavily on mobile SoCs (Snapdragon 8 Gen 2/3) and edge GPUs (Jetson Orin).
